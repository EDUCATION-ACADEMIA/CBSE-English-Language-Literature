ISWARAN THE STORYTELLER

[SOURCES]
Iswaran said that a horrible ghost of a woman appeared ____

(A) always on a new moon night.

(B) ** off and on at midnight during the full moon

(C) always on a full-moon night.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. Iswaran claimed that he had seen the ghost of a woman that appeared off and on at midnight during the full moon. He described it as an ugly creature with matted hair and a shrivelled face. It looked like a skeleton holding a foetus in its arms.

Concept note-2: -Ans. Iswaran told Mahendra that entire factory area where he worked was once a burial ground. lswaran told Mahendra that he often saw ghosts at night. Sometimes, there appeared a horrible ghost of woman.

Concept note-3: -What happens to him on a full-moon night? Answer: Mahendra calls ghosts or spirits a figment of the imagination when Iswaran tells that he has seen a female ghost holding a foetus in its arms.

Concept note-4: -Iswaran told Mahendra that factory area where they lived was a burial ground and ghosts also lived there, he told him that he had found some bones and also had seen a ghost there.
