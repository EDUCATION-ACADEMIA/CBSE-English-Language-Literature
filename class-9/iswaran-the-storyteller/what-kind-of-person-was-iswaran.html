What kind of person was Iswaran?

(A) curious learner

(B) dedicated, loyal and obedient worker

(C) multitalented full of positive traits

(D) ** all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He was very loyal and honest. He was a fascinating story teller. He had the quality as that of a television. He was well versed in the art of story telling.

Concept note-2: -Iswaran was an obedient person. He was Mahendra’s cook. He was very caring and hardworking. He did all the jobs such as cooking meal, washing clothes and chatting with Mahendra at night.

Concept note-3: -1. Iswaran was a fascinating story teller. He read a lot of story books and narrated incidents in a very vivid manner to Mahendra, his master. He could perform all kinds of actions and gestures to make the stories extremely interesting.

Concept note-4: -Ans: Iswaran was Mahendra’s cook, he was an asset to him. He cooked for him, washed his clothes and chatted away with his master at night. He often narrated stories which were very convincing.

Concept note-5: -He was dedicated to his father and followed him uncomplainingly to all his post. He would cook for him, washing clothes and also chat with him at night. He had the ability to improvise cooking material even at the remotest place and cooked delicious dish in a very short/brief time.
