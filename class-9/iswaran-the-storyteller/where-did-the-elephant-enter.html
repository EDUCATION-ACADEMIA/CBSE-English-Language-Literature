Where did the elephant enter?

(A) room

(B) house

(C) ** school ground

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The elephant now entered a school ground where children were playing, breaking through the brick wall.

Concept note-2: -Answer: One day an elephant went mad, stamping oh bushes, breaking fences, smashing fruit stalls, mud pots and clothes. He even entered a school ground breaking through the brick wall.

Concept note-3: -According to Iswaran, he was studying in the junior class at that time. He grabbed a cane from the hands of one of the teachers and ran into the open. The elephant continued grunting and stamping its feet. It looked frightening.

Concept note-4: -When the wild elephant entered the school playground, there was chaos. The boys ran into their classrooms and shut the doors. The elephant pulled out the football goalpost, flattened the drum an uprooted the shrubs.

Concept note-5: -Solution : The elephant had caused much terror, children and teachers ran here and there to save their lives. Iswaran dared it and hit it on the third toe with a rod. Thus, the mad elephant was controlled.
