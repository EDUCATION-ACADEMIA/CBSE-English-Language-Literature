ISWARAN THE STORYTELLER

[SOURCES]
“How did you bring down the beast?"What was Iswaran’s answer to this question?

(A) Yoga

(B) Kung-fu

(C) ** Karate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -lswaran told Mahendra that the elephant collapsed because of Japanese art called karate or ju-jitsu. It had temporarily paralysed the beast’s nervous system. Iswaran said that he had read about this art somewhere and used it to bring down the beast.

Concept note-2: -Ju-jitsu helped Ishwaran to bring down the beast.

Concept note-3: -What were the elements of Iswaran’s story? Answer: The elements of Iswaran’s story were a tusker and a woman ghost. The story was narrated to Ganesh by Mahendra, a supervisor in a firm.

Concept note-4: -Iswaran was Mahendra’s cook who follows his master uncomplainingly wherever he was posted. Apart from cooking, he used to wash Mahendra’s clothes, tidy up his place and chats with him at night.

Concept note-5: -He said it was called Karate or ju-jitsu. Iswaran added that he had read about it somewhere in a book. It was mentioned that a technique from the martial art would make a person unconscious and it could paralyse their body.
