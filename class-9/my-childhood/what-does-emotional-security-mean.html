What does emotional security mean?

(A) balance in life

(B) balance of emotions

(C) control over emotions

(D) ** love and care to lead a stress free life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emotional security refers to a feeling of confidence and stability in one’s emotional life. It is often associated with the ability to cope with life’s challenges and to maintain a sense of emotional well-being in the face of stress, adversity, or change.

Concept note-2: -Emotional security is the measure of the stability of an individual’s emotional state.

Concept note-3: -Ans:’Emotional security’, according to Kalam, is the love and care that one needs to lead a stress-free life.

Concept note-4: -Emotional safety enables us the freedom to collaborate, dream, be wildly creative, share bold ideas, feel increased compassion, and express ourselves freely with one another. The more we understand how our bodies play a role in our relationships, the more we can make them work for us instead of against us.
