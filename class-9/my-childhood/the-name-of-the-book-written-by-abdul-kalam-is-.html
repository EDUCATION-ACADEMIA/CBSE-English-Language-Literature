The name of the book written by Abdul Kalam is-

(A) ** Wings of Fire

(B) My Childhood

(C) My Autobiography

(D) Wings of Science

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Explanation: “Wings of Fire (Agni ki udaan)” is an autobiography of Late APJ Abdul Kalam, scientist and former president of India. The name, wings of fire was specifically chosen because he was the man behind the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology in India.

Concept note-2: -The book titled “Wings of Fire” was written by Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. It is the autobiography of the former President of India. He is also known as the Missile Man of India.

Concept note-3: -Wings of Fire is the autobiography of the former President of India, Dr. Abdul Kalam. Kalam went from being a humble boy in South India to developing India’s nuclear weapons and becoming President. Through this autobiography, the reader gains a glimpse into pre-partition India.

Concept note-4: -Wings of Fire: An Autobiography of APJ Abdul Kalam (1999) is an autobiography of A P J Abdul Kalam, former President of India. It was written by Dr. Kalam and Arun Tiwari.
