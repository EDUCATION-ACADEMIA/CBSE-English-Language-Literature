How old was Abdul Kalam when the second world war broke out in 1939

(A) eleven years

(B) nine years

(C) ten years

(D) ** eight years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kalam was only 8 years old when the second world war broke out in 1939.

Concept note-2: -The Second World War broke out in September 1939 when Nazi Germany invaded Poland, Britain and France went to Poland’s aid and declared war on Germany.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The second world war broke out in 1939 and at that time APJ Abdul kalam was 8 years old.

Concept note-4: -Solution : When the stoppage of trains was cancelled at Rameshwaram because of World War II, Kalam’s cousin, Samsuddin, who distributed newspapers in Rameswaram, asked him for help in collecting newspaper bundles which were thrown from the moving train. This helped Abdul Kalam earn his first wages.

Concept note-5: -Abdul Kalam earned his first wages by helping his cousin in catching the newspaper bundles thrown out of a moving train during the Second World War.
