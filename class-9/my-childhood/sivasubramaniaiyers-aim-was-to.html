SivasubramaniaIyer’s aim was to

(A) Humiliate Abdul

(B) ** To break the social barriers that restricted people to mingle

(C) To fight in the second world war

(D) To become rich

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Sivasubramania Iyer’s aim was to break the social barriers that restrict the people to mingle .

Concept note-2: -Solution : Kalam’s Science teacher Sivasubramaria Iyer did his best to break social barriers so that people from varying backgrounds could mingle easily.

Concept note-3: -In fact, lie tried to light against the social harries in the small town of Rameswaram. Next time when Kalam visited the teacher’s house his wife took him inside her kitchen and served him food with her own hands. Thus he tried to break social barriers in the small town of Rameswaram.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Siva Subramanya Iyer was APJ Abdul Kalam’s Science Teacher. This man played a great role in the early years of APJ Abdul Kalam’s life. He was much influenced by his science teacher for his science teacher was the one who taught him the ways of breaking down social barriers.
