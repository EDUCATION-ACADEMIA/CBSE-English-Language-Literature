Concept note-3: -His wife was horrified at the idea of a Muslim boy being invited to dine in her ritually pure kitchen. She refused to serve Kalam in her kitchen. Sivasubramania iyer was not perturbed. not did he get angry with his wife, but instead, served kalam with his own hands and sat down beside kalam to eat his meal.

Concept note-4: -Answers. Siva Subramania Iyer’s attitude towards Abdul Kalam made his wife change her mind.
