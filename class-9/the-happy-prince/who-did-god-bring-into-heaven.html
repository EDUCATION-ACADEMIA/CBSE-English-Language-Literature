CLASS 9

THE HAPPY PRINCE

[SOURCES]
Who did God bring into heaven?

(A) the little girl

(B) the little boy

(C) the poor people

(D) ** the prince and the bird

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: God rewarded the Happy Prince and the swallow by giving them a place in heaven. The swallow will sing forever in the garden of paradise and the Happy Prince will live in the city of gold.

Concept note-2: -When God asked one of the angels to bring him two precious things in the garden of paradise the angel brought the leaden heart of the prince and the dead bird.

Concept note-3: -When God asked one of the angels to bring him the two most precious things in the city, the angel brought him the leaden heart and the dead bird. God appreciates the angel’s decision and said that the little bird would forever sing in his garden of Paradise and the Happy Prince would praise him in his city of gold.

Concept note-4: -The servants of God brought the broken heart and the dead bird to God because they were the best things in the city. God has asked them to find the best things in the city and bring it to him. The Happy Prince along with the Swallow helped the people of the city to put them out of their misery.
