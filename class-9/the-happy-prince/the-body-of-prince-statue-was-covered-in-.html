CLASS 9

THE HAPPY PRINCE

[SOURCES]
the body of Prince Statue was covered in ____

(A) Twinkle Bronze

(B) Gorgeous Metal

(C) Bright Silver

(D) ** Shiny Gold

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The statue of the Happy Prince stood high above the city. It was covered with gold, its eyes were bright blue jewels, and a red jewel hung from its waist. Everyone thought that it was very beautiful.

Concept note-2: -In fact, sorrow was not allowed to enter the palace where he lived. Thus, he was called the ‘Happy Prince’ by his ministers. However, after his death, his gold and jewel-studded statue were put up on a high column in the city. From here, the Happy Prince, like a statue, got to see the pain and miseries of the people.

Concept note-3: -The happy prince wanted to make everyone’s life better so he asked swallow to take off his gold leaves and give it to the hungry and homeless children in the town.
