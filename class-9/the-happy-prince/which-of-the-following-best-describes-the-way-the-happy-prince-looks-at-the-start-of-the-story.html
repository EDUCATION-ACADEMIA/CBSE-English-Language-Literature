CLASS 9

THE HAPPY PRINCE

[SOURCES]
Which of the following best describes the way the Happy Prince looks at the start of the story?

(A) old and worn out

(B) ** bright and shiny

(C) lost and confused

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The statue of the Happy Prince stood high above the city. It was covered with gold, its eyes were bright blue jewels, and a red jewel hung from its waist. Everyone thought that it was very beautiful.

Concept note-2: -Answers. The Happy Prince is described as a beautiful statue. The statue was placed on the top of a tall pillar. The statue of the prince was completely covered with gold.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The courtiers used to call the prince ‘the Happy Prince’ because when he was alive, he did not know what tears were, for he lived in the Palace, where sorrow was not allowed to enter.

Concept note-4: -The gold leaves that covered the statue of the Happy Prince were gradually given away in charity by him. His sapphire eyes and the red ruby on the sword hilt too were given away. This made him look shabby and beggarly.
