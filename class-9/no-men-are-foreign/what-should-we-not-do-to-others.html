NO MEN ARE FOREIGN

[SOURCES]
What should we not do to others?

(A) love

(B) ** hate

(C) care

(D) meet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The most appropriate word will be b) hate. The option which will be correct is we should not hate others. There is no gain in hating others no matter what that person has done wrong.

Concept note-2: -Answer: According to the poet when we hate others we bring misery and suffering to ours and other person’s life. In a way we also hate ourselves and defile the earth with our malice thoughts of hatred and misery.

Concept note-3: -Answer: We shouldn’t Hate anyone/people if there’s no reason to hate that person/them or the issue isn’t that really bad.. Because you won’t understand them or that person easily if you’re the person who’s hating the person and you don’t know how their life is going through and how tough it is to them.

Concept note-4: -Stay away from people and places that make you uncomfortable. Take breaks away from media and social media. Engage and disengage from conversations as needed. Assert your needs and own your readiness especially if you feel judged for not being or acting a certain way.
