Whom did Santosh save with artificial respiration?

(A) Rohan Singh

(B) Sohan Singh

(C) ** Mohan Singh

(D) All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Santosh Yadav provided special care to a climber who lay dying at the south pole in 1992 Everest Mission. She saved Mohan Singh, who would have lost his life had she not shared her oxygen with him.

Concept note-2: -Mohan Singh was one of the fellow climbers of Santosh during one of her expedition to climb the Mount Everest. Mohan Singh was almost dying when Santosh saved his life by sharing her oxygen with him.

Concept note-3: -During her Everest mission in 1992, she saved the life of a climber, Mohan Singh, by sharing oxygen with him. She also tried to help a mountaineer who lay dying at the South Col but was unsuccessful in saving him.

Concept note-4: -Santosh was a fervent environmentalist. Her concern for the environment is evident from the fact that she collected and brought down 500 kilograms of garbage from the Himalayas.

Concept note-5: -Santosh asserted that her feeling at the summit of the Everest was “indescribable”. Unfurling the Indian flag on the top of the world was a spiritual moment for her and she felt proud as an Indian.
