In what words did Santosh express her feeling at the summit of her Everest climb?

(A) fabulous

(B) ** indescribable

(C) fantastic

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She says that her feelings were indescribable. It was a very great moment and she felt proud of being an Indian.

Concept note-2: -Santosh asserted that her feeling at the summit of the Everest was “indescribable”. Unfurling the Indian flag on the top of the world was a spiritual moment for her and she felt proud as an Indian.

Concept note-3: -When Santosh was at the summit of the Mount Everest her joy knew no bounds. She claimed that her feeling was ‘indescribable’. Unfurling the Indian Flag at the top was a spiritual moment for her and she felt proud as an Indian.

Concept note-4: -She wished to climb mountains when she used to watch the villagers going up the hill and suddenly vanishing after a while. One day, she decided to check it out herself. She found nobody except a few mountaineers. Later she saved money and enrolled in a course at Uttarkashi’s Nehru Institute of Mountaineering.

Concept note-5: -During the Everest expedition, her seniors in the team admired her climbing skills, physical fitness and mental strength while her concern for others and desire to work together with them endeared her to fellow climbers.
