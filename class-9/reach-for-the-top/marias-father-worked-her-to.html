[SOURCES]
Maria’s father worked her to

(A) To earn more and more

(B) To feed his kids well

(C) ** To support Maria’s training financially

(D) To plan a great vacation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maria’s parents played an important role in her success. They made it possible for Maria to start training at the young age of nine years. Her father, Yuri, took her to USA for the same. He had to work hard to keep Maria’s tennis training going on.

Concept note-2: -1 Answer. From the parents of Maria, only her father could manage to come along with her to the U.S. For the reason that Maria could keep her tennis-training going, he had to work there very much; so much so that he could hardly squeeze any time to meet Maria. This was a great sacrifice.

Concept note-3: -Ans:-Maria’s father, Yuri, had brought his nine-year-old daughter to the U.S. to get her trained in tennis. Unfortunately, he too could not see her frequently as he had to work very hard to earn to pay for her training. His work occupied most of his time and he could not see Maria often.

Concept note-4: -Her father Yuri contributed significantly in shaping her future. He managed her training in the United States. He took her from Russia to Florida, the United States. He worked hard to earn money to make Maria continue her training.
