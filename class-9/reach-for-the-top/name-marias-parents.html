Name Maria’s parents.

(A) ** Yuri and Yelena

(B) Yuri and Mitchell

(C) Adrian and Yelena

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maria Sharapova’s Parents are Yuri Sharapov and Yelena Sharapova.

Concept note-2: -Mary’s father is called Imran. He is the equivalent of Joachim in Christian tradition. Her mother, according to al-Tabari, is called Hannah, which is the same name as in Christian tradition (Saint Anne).

Concept note-3: -Yuri and Yelena Sharapova are Maria Sharapova’s father and mother respectively. Their contribution to maria’s success is immense and cannot be measured in words. They identified their daughter’s potential at a very young age and decided to get her world-class training in the U.S.

Concept note-4: -Maria’s parents played an important role in her success. They made it possible for Maria to start training at the young age of nine years. Her father, Yuri, took her to USA for the same. He had to work hard to keep Maria’s tennis training going on.
