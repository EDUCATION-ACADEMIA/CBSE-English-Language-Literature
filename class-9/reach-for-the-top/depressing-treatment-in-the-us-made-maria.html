Depressing treatment in the U.S made Maria:

(A) weak

(B) nervous

(C) timid

(D) ** tough

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maria Sharapova moves to Florida from Russia with her father at a young age to continue her tennis practices. She was bullied by her classmates since she was a foreigner. This treatment made her tough her willpower to become the best tennis player grew stronger.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Just as reaping the harvest is the reward for the hard work of the farmer, similarly earning money is the reward for Maria Sharapova’s earnest efforts and sacrifice. Tennis is a business and a sport, but the most important thing is to become number one in the world.

Concept note-3: -What is something disarming about Maria Sharapova? Maria Sharapova is a girl with a smiling face and glamorous attire. But she is a determined girl who worked excessively hard to win the world number one position in women’s tennis.

Concept note-4: -Maria was sent to the USA-The United States of America for her tennis training.

Concept note-5: -Answers. Maria Sharapova (born 1987) achieved success through hard sheer hard work and perseverance. She started tennis at the age of six and since then there has been no looking back.
