Maria felt lonely during her

(A) Time in russia

(B) ** Training

(C) Later years

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why did Maria Sharapova feel so lonely while getting her tennis training in the US? Answer: She felt lonely because her mother was compelled to stay back in Siberia because of visa restrictions thereby had to bear two years of separation from her mother.

Concept note-2: -She had to face the separation from her mother at such an early age of seven. She had to live in a hostel and undergo strict training schedules. Also, she was allowed to meet her father very occasionally. These were the reasons why she felt so lonely during her tennis training in the United States.

Concept note-3: -Marie’s childhood was full of struggle and loneliness. She had to leave for the U.S. at the young age of nine years. Her mother could not accompany her to the U.S. due to some visa restrictions. She missed her mother terribly during the two years of their heart-wrenching separation.

Concept note-4: -Maria had to stay away from her mother because, she had to go to the United States for her, professional training and her mother could not, accompany her due to visa restrictions. She felt very, , lonely and depressed about that.
