Maria belongs to which country

(A) USA

(B) Germany

(C) ** Russia

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maria Sharapova, in full Maria Yuryevna Sharapova, (born April 19, 1987, Nyagan, Russia), Russian tennis player who was one of the game’s leading contenders in the early 21st century, the winner of five Grand Slam titles.

Concept note-2: -In Russian, Masha () is a diminutive of Maria. It has been used as a nickname or as a pet name for women named Maria or Marie. An alternative spelling in the Latin alphabet is “Macha". In Serbo-Croatian and Slovene, “Maša” is a diminutive of “Marija” but can be a given name in its own right.

Concept note-3: -Though Maria Sharapova speaks with a pronounced American accent, she proudly parades her Russian nationality. Clearing all doubts, she says, “I’m Russian. It’s true that the U.S. is a big part of my life. But I have Russian citizenship.
