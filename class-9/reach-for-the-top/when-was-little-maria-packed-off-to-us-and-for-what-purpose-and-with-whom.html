When was little Maria packed off to U.S and for what purpose and with whom?

(A) at the age of 9

(B) at the age of 14

(C) at the age of 15

(D) ** at the age of 10 for training in tennis with her father

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Explanation: Little Maria had not yet celebrated her tenth birthday when she was packed off to train in the United States. That trip to Florida with her father Yuri launched her on the path to success and stardom.

Concept note-2: -Answer: 4.] at the age of 9 for training in tennis with her father.

Concept note-3: -Maria was sent to the United States for her tennis training. Her mother could not go with her because of visa restrictions.

Concept note-4: -11. What important lesson did Maria learn during the first two years of her stay in the U.S., away from her mother? Maria learnt that excellence comes at a price. Since she wished to reach the zenith in the field of tennis, she knew that she would have to make sacrifices and struggle hard.

Concept note-5: -Answer. The most important thing for Maria was to earn money.
