THE SNAKE AND THE MIRROR

[SOURCES]
Who all are the main character of the story?

(A) SNAKE

(B) MIRROR

(C) DOCTOR

(D) ** ALL OF THE ABOVE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The protagonist is the character who drives the action–the character whose fate matters most. In other words, they are involved in-and often central to-the plot or conflict of the story, but are also usually the emotional heart of the narrative.

Concept note-2: -“The protagonist is the character whose fate matters most to the story.”

Concept note-3: -Manuel, the neighborhood baker, bakes delicious smelling pastries and puts them out for sale each morning. His neighbor, Pablo, loves to smell the fresh bakery every morning but he never purchases any pastries. This irritates Manuel to the point where he feels Pablo should pay to smell his baked goods.

Concept note-4: -It’s a story about Allison and her schizophrenic mother who lost her mind when the love of her life didn’t come back to her in Ireland.
