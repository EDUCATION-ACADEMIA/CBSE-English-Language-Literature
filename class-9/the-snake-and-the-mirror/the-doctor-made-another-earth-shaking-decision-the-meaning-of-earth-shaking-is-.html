THE SNAKE AND THE MIRROR

[SOURCES]
The doctor made another earth shaking decision. The meaning of earth shaking is ____

(A) ** challenging

(B) dangerous

(C) horrible

(D) pleasant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(c) important. The earth-shaking means very important, shocking, or traumatic.

Concept note-2: -The earth-shaking decision made by the doctor was that he would always keep that attractive smile on his face.

Concept note-3: -What two “important” and “earth-shaking” decisions did the doctor take while he was looking into the mirror? Ans: The doctor first decided to shave daily and grow a thin moustache and secondly, he decided to keep an attractive smile always on his lips.

Concept note-4: -The doctor wants to marry a rich woman doctor. He also wants his wife to be fat, who would not be able to catch hold of him if someday he does something silly and has to run away from her. But, contrary to his wishes, he ends up getting married to a slim lady who could run like a “sprinter”.

Concept note-5: -The doctor took two “important” and “earth-shaking” decisions while he was looking into the mirror. These were: First, he decided to shave daily and grow a thin moustache. Second, he would always keep an attractive smile on his face.
