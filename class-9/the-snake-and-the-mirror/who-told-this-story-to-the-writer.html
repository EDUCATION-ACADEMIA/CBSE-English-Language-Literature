THE SNAKE AND THE MIRROR

[SOURCES]
Who told this story to the writer?

(A) His granny

(B) his mother

(C) his father

(D) ** a homeopathic doctor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is a story about a homoeopathic doctor during his early career days. The narrator of this story is the doctor himself, where he narrates this story to some individuals.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The narrator of the story is a doctor. He is telling this story to some of his friends.

Concept note-3: -Character Sketch of the doctor The doctor is the main character of the story. He practises homeopath and is obsessed with his own looks. He is also proud of the fact that he is a doctor. He appears to be a man full of vanity as we see him looking into the mirror and admiring his own looks.

Concept note-4: -“Has a snake ever coiled itself round any part of your body?”-> Who asked the question? Answer: Homeopath.

Concept note-5: -Snake and The Mirror summary is the real story of the doctor and the realization of his true worth. After that, the snake left the doctor and further moved towards the mirror. The doctor silently ran from there and saved his life. After this everyone just loved the Snake and The Mirror story summary.
