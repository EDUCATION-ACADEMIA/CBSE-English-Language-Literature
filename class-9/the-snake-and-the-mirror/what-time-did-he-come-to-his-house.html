What time did he come to his house?

(A) 10’o clock in morning

(B) 2’o clock in afternoon

(C) 6’o clock in evening

(D) ** 10’o clock in night

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The use of the Prepositions “at” and “in” is correct in the the sentence “ Call me at 10’o clock in the morning” “at” is used when mentioning a specific time.

Concept note-2: -Late-afternoon: 3-6 p.m. Evening: 6-9 p.m. Late at night: Midnight-6 a.m.

Concept note-3: -(Only in the 12-hour clock) The start of the twenty-third hour of the day; 10:00 p.m. (22:00).

Concept note-4: -AT a time (times: 10 o’clock, 5:30, etc.)
