The grandfather traveled from where to where to collect his pension.

(A) Nagpur to Sharanpur

(B) Nainital to Dibrugarh

(C) New Delhi to Nainital

(D) ** Sharanpur to Dehradun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grandfather had to leave Dehra Dun the next day to collect his pension in Saharanpur.

Concept note-2: -He did not allow other pet animals to sleep at night. Thus, Grandfather decided to take Toto to Saharanpur. A big black canvas bag was arranged for Toto to travel.

Concept note-3: -Toto was transferred to a big cage in the servants’ quarters where a number of grandfather’s pets lived together. However, Toto would not allow other animals to sleep at night. Therefore, grandfather, who had to leave for Dehradun and Saharanpur to collect his pension, decided to take it along with him.

Concept note-4: -Describe the situation in which grandfather found himself at the Saharanpur railway station. Ans. Toto’s presence had still not been disclosed to the grandmother. The next day, grandfather had to go to Saharanpur to get his pension.
