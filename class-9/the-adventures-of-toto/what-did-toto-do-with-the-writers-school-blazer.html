What did Toto do with the writer’s school blazer?

(A) He threw it out of the window.

(B) ** He tore it into shreds.

(C) He wore it on his back.

(D) He used it as his bedding.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: He tore it into shreds.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Grandfather was pleased with toto’sperformance. He described him as clever and deduced that if he had been given more time, he would have tied the torn pieces of the blazer into a rope and made his escape through the window.

Concept note-3: -What did Toto do with the writer’s school blazer? a)He tore it into shredsb)He wore it on his backc)He threw it out of the windowd)He made it into his beddingCorrect answer is option ‘A’.

Concept note-4: -Toto tore off the ornamental paper that covered the wall of the narrator’s room and wrenched the peg, to which he had been tied, from its socket. He also tore the narrator’s school blazer into shreds.
