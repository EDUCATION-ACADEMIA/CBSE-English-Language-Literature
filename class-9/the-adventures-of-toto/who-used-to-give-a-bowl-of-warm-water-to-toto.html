Who used to give a bowl of warm water to Toto?

(A) writer

(B) ** grandmother

(C) grandfather

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: (b) Grandmother gave him a large bowl of warm water.

Concept note-2: -A great treat for Toto during cold winter evenings was the large bowl of warm water given him by Grandmother for his bath. He would cunningly test the temperature with his hand, then gradually step into the bath, first one foot, then the other (as he had seen me doing), until he was into the water up to his neck.

Concept note-3: -Solution : Toto takes bath in a tub of warm water. It puts its legs in the water one by one andapplies soap as well. As monkeys are good at aping others, so Toto has learnt proper steps of bathing while watching the narrator doing same. Toto is fond of bathing with warm water.

Concept note-4: -Answer: by dipping his toe in the water, he checked whether the temperature of the water is moderate or not.

Concept note-5: -Explanation: The water was so hot that when Grandma gave it to Toto, it burned Toto.
