What did Toto love in cold winter evenings?

(A) A spicy samosa

(B) A dish of pullao

(C) ** A warm bath

(D) A cup of tea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Toto used to love taking a bath in hot water during the cold weather. A great treat for Toto during cold winter evenings was the large bowl of warm water given him by Grandmother for his bath.

Concept note-2: -Ans : Taking bath in warm water in cold winter evenings was a great treat for Toto.

Concept note-3: -If you like to bathe early in the morning, a cold water bath is advisable. However, if you like to bathe in the evening, try a hot water shower. As the evening is dominated by the Vata dosha, hence it is ideal.

Concept note-4: -When the water became cold, he would get out and run quickly to the kitchen fire in order to dry himself. Toto learnt to do so as he had seen the narrator do the same.

Concept note-5: -Given below is the answer to your question. For Toto, bathing in a large bowl full of warm water during the cold winters was a treat for him. Toto would very cunningly check the temperature of the water which the grandmother used to bring for him to the bath.
