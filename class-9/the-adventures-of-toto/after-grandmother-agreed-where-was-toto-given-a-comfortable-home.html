Concept note-1: -When Toto was finally accepted by Grandmother he was given a comfortable home in the stable, where he had for a companion the family donkey, Nana. On Toto’s first night in the stable, Grandfather paid him a visit to see if he was comfortable.

Concept note-2: -Toto did not get along well with other animals. He troubled Nana, the family donkey. He often fastened himself onto her long ears with his sharp teeth. He did not allow other animals to sleep in peace.

Concept note-3: -Toto was given a position in a stable after Grandmother accepted his presence in their house. Grandfather kept it with their family donkey, Nana, because Toto wouldn’t let other animals sleep through the night.
