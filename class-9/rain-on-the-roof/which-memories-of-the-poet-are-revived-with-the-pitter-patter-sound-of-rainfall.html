CLASS 9

RAIN ON THE ROOF

[SOURCES]
Which memories of the poet are revived with the pitter patter sound of rainfall?

(A) college days

(B) young age

(C) office days

(D) ** childhood memories

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: This rain and its sound have resurrected the fondest memory of the poet’s mother in his mind.

Concept note-2: -Which distinctive memory is revived in the poet’s mind? The single major memory that comes distinctly to the poet or his mother. The rain revives his thoughts of the time when his mother used to look lovingly at him and his siblings before she went to sleep in her bedchamber.

Concept note-3: -The poet remembers his childhood when he stood alone in the courtyard listening to the music of cymbal. He regrets that the days gone by do not return. (companion’s) hand with love.

Concept note-4: -The poet’s mother is the single major memory that comes to his mind. ‘Darling dreamers’ are those ‘kids’ who remember their mothers like the poet. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-5: -Answer: The single major memory that comes to the poet is that of his mother. The “darling dreamers” are those ‘kids’ who remember their mothers like the poet. 4.
