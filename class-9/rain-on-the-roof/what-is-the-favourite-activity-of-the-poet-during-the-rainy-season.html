RAIN ON THE ROOF

[SOURCES]
What is the favourite activity of the poet during the rainy season?

(A) listening to the music

(B) eating snacks

(C) roaming on the roof

(D) ** lie on the bed and listen to the pitter-patter sound of the rain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Expert-Verified Answer. The favourite activity of the poet during the rainy season is (d) lying on the bed and listening to the sound of the raindrops. what do poets do when it’s raining? This is from the poem “Rain on the Roof” written by the poet Coates Kinney.

Concept note-2: -The poet listens to the patter caused by the sounds of the raindrops on the roof. At this moment the memory of his mother comes alive into his mind.

Concept note-3: -AI Recommended Answer: likely to be listening to the pitter patter sound of rain.

Concept note-4: -Lying in his bed, the poet is listening to the pattering sound of the rain falling on the roof.

Concept note-5: -2. What Does the Poet Like To Do When It Rains? Ans: When it rains, the poet enjoys lying in his rustic chamber bed with his head tucked against the pillow and listening to the quiet rain patter.
