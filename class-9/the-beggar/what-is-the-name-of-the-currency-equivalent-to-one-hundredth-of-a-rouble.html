What is the name of the currency equivalent to one hundredth of a rouble

(A) paise

(B) ** copecks

(C) francs

(D) fils

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ruble or rouble (Russian: rublʹ; symbol: ₽; abbreviation: or . in Cyrillic, Rub in Latin; ISO code: RUB) is the currency of the Russian Federation. The ruble is subdivided into 100 kopecks (sometimes written as copeck or kopek; Russian: ́, romanized: kopeyka, pl. ́, kopeyki).

Concept note-2: -The ruble is subdivided into 100 kopeks (or kopecks, copecks).

Concept note-3: -The kopeck is the smallest Russian currency unit still in circulation today. One kopeck is equivalent to one hundredth of a ruble and one ruble is currently worth 0.011€. However, some kopeck coins can have a good value because this monetary unit is very old.

Concept note-4: -The Russian ruble is divided into 100 kopecks. The Central Bank of the Russian Federation has the exclusive authority to issue banknotes and coins in Russia.
