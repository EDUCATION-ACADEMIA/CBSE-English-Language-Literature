What was Gerrard’s full name?

(A) Vincent Bill Gerrard

(B) Vincent William Gerrard

(C) Vincent Henry Gerrard

(D) ** Vincent Charles Gerrard

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gerrard’s full name was (d) Vincent Charles Gerrard.

Concept note-2: -Here is the answer: The full name of Gerrard is Vincent Charles Gerrard.

Concept note-3: -Gerrard is associated with the theatre. He writes, acts and provides props for plays. He is a refined and a cultured man, who keeps his cool even in the most difficult situations. The sight of an intruder doesn’t ruffle him and he talks to him very courteously and pleasantly.

Concept note-4: -Ans: Gerrard’s profession is that of a playwright. There are number of instances that reflect this but some of them are as follows: This is all very melodramatic, not very original, perhaps, but…” • Finally, a sympathetic audience!”
