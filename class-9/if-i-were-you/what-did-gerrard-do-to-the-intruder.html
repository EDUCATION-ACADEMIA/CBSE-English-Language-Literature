What did Gerrard do to the intruder?

(A) he killed him

(B) he knocked him down

(C) ** he locked him up in a cupboard

(D) all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When the intruder leaned forward to see out, Gerrard immediately pushed him into the cupboard and slammed the door and locked it. He then picked the intruder’s revolver and kept it pointed towards the cupboard. In this way, Gerrard trapped the intruder.

Concept note-2: -Gerrard opened the door and stopped away. The intruder leaned forward to inspect it with his sight (eye-sight) towards Gerrard but with his revolver ready. As turned his head, Gerrard gave him a push and trapped him in the cupboard. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Answer: The mystery that Gerrard proposes to explain is a story that he makes to fool the intruder by making him believe that he is also a dangerous man. He tells him that he himself is a criminal. He asks the intruder that why doesn’t he meet any tradespeople and be a bit of a mystery man here today and gone tomorrow.

Concept note-4: -Gerrard tricked the intruder into believing that they had to leave the house right away. Then he opened the door and let the intruder look outside. While the intruder was peeping out of the door, Gerrard pushed him. The push landed the intruder in the cupboard and the revolver out of the intruder’s hand.
