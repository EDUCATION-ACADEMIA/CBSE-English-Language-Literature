Do you think Gerrard was actually a murder?

(A) Yes

(B) ** No

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Do you think Gerrard was actually a murderer? a)Yes b)Noc)Maybed)Not knownCorrect answer is option ‘B’. Can you explain this answer? Option B No, he was not a murderer actually he was just acting to make the intruder fool also he was actually an actor by profession.

Concept note-2: -Gerrard is more intelligent becoz he did not get trap in intruder’s plan. With the help of his quick and clever thinking he got rid of intruder and trapped intruder in his own plan. This shows that quick and clever thinking is the solution of every problem.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The Intruder does not kill Gerrard immediately because he first wants to get all the necessary information from him. Without this information, his plan to take on Gerrard’s identity will not succeed.

Concept note-4: -Ans: Gerrard’s remark ‘you have been so modest’ was ironical. The intruder had been boasting of his intelligence and smartness. Hence, Gerrard taunted him about his modesty and asked him to say something about himself.

Concept note-5: -Gerrard told the Intruder that he lived alone in the lonely cottage (in Essex). Few people know about him and visit him. He is like a mystery man like the Intruder. He had killed a person and the police was after him.
