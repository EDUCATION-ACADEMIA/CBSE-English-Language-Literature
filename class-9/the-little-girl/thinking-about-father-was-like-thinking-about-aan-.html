THE LITTLE GIRL

[SOURCES]
Thinking about father was like thinking about a/an ____

(A) elf

(B) king

(C) ** giant

(D) prince

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. Kezia felt that her father was like a giant because he had very big hands and neck. His mouth seemed big, especially when he yawned. In addition, his stern and cold behaviour too made the little girl thinks of him as a giant.

Concept note-2: -Now, Kezia realised that her father was not as harsh as she thought. She realised that her father had to work all day long and got so tired that he could not play with her. She realised that her father wanted her to understand his compulsion. At the bottom, he was a very good person.

Concept note-3: -Kezia avoided her father because he was very strict and always used to give commands. She was afraid because he never played with her and spoke to her lovingly and calmly.

Concept note-4: -Ans-Kezia was afraid of her father because he was very strict who always used to give commands to everybody else in the house. He never played with her.
