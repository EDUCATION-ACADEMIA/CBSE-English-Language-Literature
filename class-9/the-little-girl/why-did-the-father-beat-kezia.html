Why did the father beat kezia?

(A) She back-answered

(B) She did not do the task assigned to her

(C) She misbehaved

(D) ** kezia tore the fathers speech to make a gift for him

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This was because the sheets she had torn were her father’s speech for the Port Authority. Her father scolded her for touching things that did not belong to her and punished her by hitting her palm with a ruler. 2. Kezia decides that there are “different kinds of fathers”.

Concept note-2: -She tore important papers of her father to stuff in the pin cushion because she was making a gift to present to her father. Since the papers had her father’s speech written on it, she was punished for tearing it.

Concept note-3: -One day when her father looked for the papers, he did not find them. After some time, he came to know that Kezia had torn them into pieces to make a pin-cushion. He got infuriated and beat her with a ruler. Thus, her efforts to please her father resulted in displeasing him very much.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Kezia was beaten by her father severely because she had torn out his important papers. She wept very badly and ran towards her grandmother. She was very upset and angry towards her father.

Concept note-5: -Ans: Kezia once earned her father’s wrath for tearing his speech for the Port Authority to stuff a pin-cushion she was making for him as a birthday present. When he discovered that Kezia was the culprit, he punished her by beating her little pink palms with a ruler to teach her not to touch what did not belong to her.
