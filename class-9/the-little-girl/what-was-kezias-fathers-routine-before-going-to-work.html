What was Kezia’s father’s routine before going to work?

(A) ** He would come into her room and give her a casual goodbye kiss.

(B) He would ask for his boots.

(C) He would ask for a newspaper.

(D) He would request for a cup of tea.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: (i) Before going to office the father used to give a casual kiss and asked her to say goodbye. (ii) After coming back from office he would ask for newspaper and a cup of tea. (iii) On Sundays he would sleep on the sofa with his face covered with his favourite cushion.

Concept note-2: -Answer: (i) Before going to his office, Kezia’s father usually went into her room to give her a casual kiss. (ii) After coming back from his office, he ordered for tea to be brought into the drawing room. (iii)On Sunday, Kezia’s father would stretch out on the sofa.

Concept note-3: -The daily routine of Kezia’s father was to visit her daughter’s room, meet her, and give her a kiss before leaving for work. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -(iii) On Sunday, Kezia’s father would stretch out on the sofa. He would cover his face with his handkerchief, put his feet on one of the cushions and sleep soundly.

Concept note-5: -He would ask for the newspaper and tea after coming back from his office.
