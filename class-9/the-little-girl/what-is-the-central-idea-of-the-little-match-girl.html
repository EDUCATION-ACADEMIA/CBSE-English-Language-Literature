What is the central idea of, “The Little Match Girl”?

(A) Wishing on a falling star made the little girls dreams come true.

(B) ** The little poor girl never gave up hope.

(C) Having food and warmth is what saved the little girl.

(D) Poor children don’t have the same luxuries as rich children.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. What is the theme of, “ The Little Match Girl"? Learn to take care of yourself. Greed is necessary to survive. Never lose hope because it can guide you and help solve your problems.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: By Hans Christian Anderson, “The Little Match Girl” is a story about a girl who freezes to death on the street after experiences beautiful visions are she watches the last of her matches burn out. The main idea of “The Little Match Girl” is a criticism of Western work ethic.

Concept note-3: -In portraying the innocent girl’s helpless, futile attempts to escape her difficult circumstances, Andersen shows that poverty is brutal, inescapable, and ultimately a worse fate than death.

Concept note-4: -In the story, The Little Match Girl, ’ the girl had the hope that she would pull through the difficulties and therefore, she continued to keep herself warm by lighting the matches. She had faith in God that he would help her.
