Why was the little girl asked to make a present for her father?

(A) ** his birthday was approaching next week

(B) he was going on a tour

(C) his anniversary was coming

(D) he was going to retire

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kezia was sick with a cold, so she was supposed to stay at home. So, since she was at home and her father’s birthday was approaching, her grandmother suggested she make him a birthday gift. She asked Keziato to make a pin cushion for him and gave her a yellow silk fabric to make the cushion out of.

Concept note-2: -How did this happen? Answer: Kezia’s grandmother asked her to make some surprise gift for the father’s birthday. She wanted to make a nice pin cushion for her father.

Concept note-3: -Answer is a pin-cushion because her grandmother suggested her to give a pin-cushion to his father, as his father birthday was coming.

Concept note-4: -Solution : She asked Kezia to make a pin-cushion as a birthday gift for her father. Kezia made the cushion by stitching three sides of the cushion with double cotton. She then filled it with papers which she got it from her mother‘s room bed-table.

Concept note-5: -Solution : Kezia’s grandmother encouraged her to get to know her father better by sending her to the drawing room to talk to her parents on Sundays. She also suggested Kezia to make a pin cushion out of a beautiful piece of yellow silk as a gift for her father’s birthday.
