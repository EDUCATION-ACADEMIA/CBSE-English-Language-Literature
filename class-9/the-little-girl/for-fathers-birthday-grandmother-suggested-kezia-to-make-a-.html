For father’s birthday, grandmother suggested Kezia to make a ____

(A) birthday card

(B) cake

(C) quilt

(D) ** pin-cushion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution : Kezia’s grandmother encouraged her to get to know her father better by sending her to the drawing room to talk to her parents on Sundays. She also suggested Kezia to make a pin cushion out of a beautiful piece of yellow silk as a gift for her father’s birthday.

Concept note-2: -Kezia’s grandmother sent her to her father’s room to have a nice talk with him. Besides, she asked her to make a gift of a pin-cushion on her father’s birthday.

Concept note-3: -Solution : Kezia made a pin-cushion as a birthday gift for her father. In order to prepare it, she laboriously stitched its three sides with double cotton and stuffed it with papers that she took from the bed-table in her mother’s room.

Concept note-4: -Solution : She asked Kezia to make a pin-cushion as a birthday gift for her father. Kezia made the cushion by stitching three sides of the cushion with double cotton. She then filled it with papers which she got it from her mother‘s room bed-table.

Concept note-5: -Solution : Kezia’s grandmother ask her to make a gift of pin cushion for her father’s birthday.
