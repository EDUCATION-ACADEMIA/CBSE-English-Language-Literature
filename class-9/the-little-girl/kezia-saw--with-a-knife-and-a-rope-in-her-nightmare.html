CLASS 9

THE LITTLE GIRL

[SOURCES]
Kezia saw ____ with a knife and a rope in her nightmare.

(A) a priest

(B) ** a butcher

(C) a chef

(D) a vegetable vendor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kezia often used to have a nightmare in which she saw a butcher who had a knife with him. The butcher also had a rope with him. His smile was very dreadful which made Kezia wake up in fear. Her father helped her when she shrieked out in fear of the nightmare.

Concept note-2: -So now this is a nightmare that Kezia had often-she would see a butcher. So this butcher would hold a big knife-a huge butcher’s knife and a rope and he would approach Kezia. He had a dreadful smile on his face. And once Kezia would see him, she would feel that she was unable to move.

Concept note-3: -Passage: “Oh, a butcher-a knife-I want Grannie.” Explanation of the above Passage: Kezia was still scared of the nightmare. She said that there was a butcher, he was holding a dreadful knife and she wanted her grandmother.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Kezia’s nightmare was that a butcher with a knife and a rope was coming close to him with a weird and scary smile.
