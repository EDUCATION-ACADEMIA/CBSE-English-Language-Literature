[SOURCES]
Kezia felt her father was harder than her

(A) ** Grandmother

(B) The Port Authority

(C) The police inspector

(D) Headmistress

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She then realized that her father was actually tender and affectionate and this changed her perception towards him.

Concept note-2: -Ans: Being afraid of her father, Kezia hesitated to speak to him. Whenever she had to, she would stutter and sound different because her natural speech would be obstructed. He was so big – his hands and his neck, especially his mouth when he yawned. Thinking about him alone was like thinking about a giant.

Concept note-3: -Ans. Kezia felt that her father was like a giant because he had very big hands and neck. His mouth seemed big, especially when he yawned. In addition, his stern and cold behaviour too made the little girl thinks of him as a giant.

Concept note-4: -Now she felt that her father was a poor man who worked very hard and so, he got so tired that he went off to sleep and did not play with her like Mr. Macdonald did with his children. But Kezia felt that her father’s heart was also full of love for her.
