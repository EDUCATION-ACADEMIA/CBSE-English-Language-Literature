How did Kezia feel when she had to speak to her father?

(A) joyful

(B) confident

(C) ** she would stutter while speaking to him

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Note: Kezia was terrified of her father, and she stuttered when she spoke to him. This irritated him tremendously.

Concept note-2: -Kezia’s father always scolded her and never spoke to her in a loving manner. Thus, she was afraid of him and found it difficult to speak in front of him. So, Kezia stammered in front of her father.

Concept note-3: -Kezia was very scared of her father and stuttered while talking to him.

Concept note-4: -Answer: kezia feels scared when her father stutters on her.

Concept note-5: -One day when her father looked for the papers, he did not find them. After some time, he came to know that Kezia had torn them into pieces to make a pin-cushion. He got infuriated and beat her with a ruler. Thus, her efforts to please her father resulted in displeasing him very much.
