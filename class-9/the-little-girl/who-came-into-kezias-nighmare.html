Who came into Kezia’s nighmare?

(A) Joker

(B) Ghost

(C) Kezia’s Father

(D) ** Butcher

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Kezia often had a nightmare. In the nightmare she often saw a butcher with a knife and a rope, who came nearer and nearer, smiling that dreadful smile; while she could not move, could only stand still, crying out, “Grandma!

Concept note-2: -Although Kezia tried sleeping, the same old nightmare came back to her. In it, she saw a butcher with a knife and rope approaching her, bearing an evil smile. She was so scared that she could only stand up and cry out.

Concept note-3: -Kezia often used to have a nightmare in which she saw a butcher with a knife. The butcher also had a rope with him. His smile was very dreadful, which made Kezia wake up in fear. Her father helped her when she shrieked out in fear of the nightmare.

Concept note-4: -He tells Butcher that everyone that’s ever loved him, dies because of his actions. He then proceeds to kill himself with the gun. Butcher carried a lot of guilt for leaving his brother and all of it manifested in this nightmare.

Concept note-5: -So now this is a nightmare that Kezia had often-she would see a butcher. So this butcher would hold a big knife-a huge butcher’s knife and a rope and he would approach Kezia. He had a dreadful smile on his face. And once Kezia would see him, she would feel that she was unable to move.
