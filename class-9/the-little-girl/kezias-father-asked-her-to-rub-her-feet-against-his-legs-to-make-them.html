CLASS 9

THE LITTLE GIRL

[SOURCES]
Kezia’s father asked her to rub her feet against his legs to make them

(A) Small

(B) Big

(C) ** Warm

(D) Cold

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Her father scolded her for touching things that did not belong to her and punished her by hitting her palm with a ruler. 2. Kezia decides that there are “different kinds of fathers”.

Concept note-2: -Kezia’s father was a hard-working but short-tempered man. He was a strict disciplinarian too. When Kezia’s mother was hospitalized, she was alone in her room. Her father comforted her when she was afraid.

Concept note-3: -(i) Before going to his office, Kezia’s father went into her room to give her a casual kiss. (ii) After coming back from his office, he would ask for newspaper and tea.

Concept note-4: -Which expressions on Kezia’s face annoyed Father? Ans. Besides her stuttering, the expressions of gloom and wretchedness on Kezia’s face annoyed Father. He felt that with such expressions, she seemed as if she were on the verge of suicide.
