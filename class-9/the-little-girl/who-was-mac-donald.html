Who was Mac Donald?

(A) Kezia’s uncle

(B) Kezia’s teacher

(C) Kezia’s grandfather

(D) ** Kezia’s neighbor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans: The Macdonalds were Kezia’s neighbours. She observed that Mr Macdonald played cheerfully with his children. He laughed when they turned the hose on him and ran about flower-beds with his young son, Mao, on his shoulders and his two little daughters hanging on to his coat pockets.

Concept note-2: -Mr McDonald was the neighbour of kezia he was a very big caring and loving father . he played games along with his five childrens. kezia and never found him scolding his children or punishing them like his father Kezia wished that his father was like McDonald who could give some time to his children.

Concept note-3: -She found him very cruel and harsh. On the other hand, she liked her neighbour, Mr Macdonald who had five children. She found him always smiling, playing, laughing and running with his children. Seeing them playing and laughing, she thought that there were different sorts of fathers.

Concept note-4: -Kezia considered Mr. Macdonald to be a very soft-hearted and loving father in comparison to her father. He was very gentle in his approach and had a healthy relationship with his children.
