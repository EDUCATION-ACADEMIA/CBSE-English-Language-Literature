What was Kezia’s father’s routine after coming back from his office?

(A) ** He would ask for the newspaper and tea after coming back from his office.

(B) He would ask for cold coffee

(C) He would ask for glass of water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After coming back from the office, Kezia’s father had a daily routine set for himself. After coming back, he asked her mother to bring some tea for him. He also asked her to bring him his slippers and the newspaper. He asked Kezia to pull off his boots.

Concept note-2: -He would ask for the newspaper and tea after coming back from his office.

Concept note-3: -Answer: (i) Before going to his office, Kezia’s father usually went into her room to give her a casual kiss. (ii) After coming back from his office, he ordered for tea to be brought into the drawing room. (iii)On Sunday, Kezia’s father would stretch out on the sofa.

Concept note-4: -The daily routine of Kezia’s father was to visit her daughter’s room, meet her, and give her a kiss before leaving for work. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-5: -When Kezia’s father returned home from the office she was supposed to come down, take off her father’s shoes, and put them outside. Was this answer helpful?
