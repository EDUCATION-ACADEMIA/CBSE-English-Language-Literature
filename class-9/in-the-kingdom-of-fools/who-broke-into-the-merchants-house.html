Who broke into the merchant’s house?

(A) king

(B) ** a thief

(C) minister

(D) guru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: thief broke into a rich merchant’s house; and as he was sneaking out of the house with the plunder through the hole, the wall collapsed and he died. The thief’s brother demanded justice from the king; he accused the merchant of getting a poor quality wall built that killed his brother.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Thief broke into the merchant house.

Concept note-3: -One bright day, a thief broke into a rich merchant’s house. He had made a hole in the wall and sneaked in, and as he was carrying out his loot, the wall of the old house collapsed on his head and killed him on the spot.

Concept note-4: -Answer. Answer: Burglars broke into the famous ‘Gajanan Parotha House’ in Junagadh’s Gandhi Chowk. However, instead of stealing money, all they did was cook food, eat, and leave.

Concept note-5: -It made him fatter than he had been previously. Later, on a bright day, a thief came to steal things from a merchant’s house. Since the people in the foolish kingdom would sleep during the day, the burglar would prefer to steal at that time.
