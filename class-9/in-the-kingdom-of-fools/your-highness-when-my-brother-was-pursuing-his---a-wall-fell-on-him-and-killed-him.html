CLASS 9

IN THE KINGDOM OF FOOLS

[SOURCES]
“Your Highness, when my brother was pursuing his ____ , a wall fell on him and killed him."

(A) Burglary

(B) ** Ancient Trade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -His brother ran to the king and complained, “Your Highness, when my brother was pursuing his ancient trade, a wall fell on him and killed him. This merchant is to blame. He should have built a good, strong wall. You must punish the wrongdoer and compensate the family for this injustice.”

Concept note-2: -Answer: The ancient trade mentioned in the chapter The Kingdom of Fools was the profession of burglary. A burglar was in the process of breaking into the house of a merchant when the wall fell on him and killed him.

Concept note-3: -In ancient times, trade began as a barter system in which people exchanged one object for another. Prehistoric humans traded animal skins or services for food. Over time, coins and currencies began to emerge.

Concept note-4: -The brother of the thief complained to the king that the rich man was responsible for the thief’s death because the wall of his house was weak. The owner of the house was summoned. But he said that the bricklayer who had constructed the wall was responsible.

Concept note-5: -The disciple stayed on, ate his fill every day-bananas and ghee and rice and wheat, and grew fat like a street-side sacred bull. One bright day, a thief broke into a rich merchant’s house.
