IN THE KINGDOM OF FOOLS

[SOURCES]
It was decided to hang the disciple in place of the merchant because

(A) He was the real culprit

(B) He had not followed his guru’s advice

(C) He was living in the town illegally

(D) ** He was fat enough to fit the stake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(d) The disciple was hanged in place of the merchant because he was fat enough to fit the stake. Answer: (d) The disciple was hanged in place of the merchant because he was fat enough to fit the stake.

Concept note-2: -Answer:The king had ordered the execution of the rich merchant. Since the rich merchant could not be executed the disciple was chosen to fit the stake. The disciple’s Guru had a vision and saw everything.

Concept note-3: -Why was it decided to execute the disciple? The rich man was too thin to be executed properly on the stake. A fat man was needed for this. So the disciple was chosen to be executed.

Concept note-4: -Answer: (c)’the merchant was too thin to be properly executed on the stake.
