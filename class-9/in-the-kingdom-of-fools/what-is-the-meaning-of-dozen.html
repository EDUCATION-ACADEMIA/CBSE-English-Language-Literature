What is the meaning of “dozen”?

(A) A group or set of 10

(B) A group or set of 5

(C) ** A group or set of 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A group of 12 objects or items are collectively called ‘a dozen’ items. Thus, a dozen bananas means 12 bananas. The word dozen comes from the French word ‘douzaine’ which means a group of 12.

Concept note-2: -A “dozen” is a unit of measurement. It means twelve (12) items of something. The term goes back to duodecim, which means 12 in Latin. Humans might have started to count on a base 12 because there are approximately 12 cycles of the moon in one cycle of the sun.

Concept note-3: -If you have a dozen things, you have twelve of them. You will be able to take ten dozen bottles free of duty through customs. You can refer to a group of approximately twelve things or people as a dozen. You can refer to a group of approximately six things or people as half a dozen.

Concept note-4: -: a group of 12. : an indefinitely large number.

Concept note-5: -Twelve dozen are called a gross (12² = 144); a dozen gross is known as a great gross (12³ = 1728), a term used in wholesale trade.
