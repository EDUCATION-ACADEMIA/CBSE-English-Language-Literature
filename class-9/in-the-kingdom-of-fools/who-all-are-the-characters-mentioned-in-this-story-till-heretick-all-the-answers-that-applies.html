[SOURCES]
Who all are the characters mentioned in this story till here?Tick all the answers that applies?

(A) ** Guru

(B) Two Disciples

(C) ** The dancing girl

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One discovers that the dancing girl has no feet. She is small, a little over 10 cm tall-the length of a human palm-but she surprises us with the power of great art – the ability to communicate across centuries.

Concept note-2: -She speaks of the undaunted, ever hopeful human spirit.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Dancing Girl’ is a sculpture made of bronze. It belongs to the Indus Valley Civilization and dates back to circa 2500 BCE. It is 10.5 cm in height, 5 cm in width and 2.5 cm in depth. Presently, it is on display in the Indus Valley Civilization gallery in the National Museum, New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -NCERT Class 7 English Solutions for Exercise Think about it in Chapter 1-Chapter-1 The Tiny Teacher. What jobs are new ants trained for? Answer: New ants are trained by the old ants on the jobs of workers, cleaners, soldiers, builders, etc.
