CLASS 9

IN THE KINGDOM OF FOOLS

[SOURCES]
Who left the city?

(A) disciple

(B) ** guru

(C) silly minister

(D) king

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The Guru’s words of wisdom were that it was the city of fools and their unpredictable behaviour could create danger anytime. He advised the disciple to leave the city because he would not know what they would do next. The disciple remembers these words when he was going to be executed in place of the merchant.

Concept note-2: -One day a guru and his disciple arrived in the city. It was a beautiful city, it was broad daylight, but there was no one about. Everyone was asleep, not a mouse stirring. Even the cattle had been taught to sleep by day.

Concept note-3: -Who visited the Kingdom of fools? Guruji and his disciples visited the kingdom of fools.

Concept note-4: -The story ‘In the Kingdom of Fools’, is about a kingdom where the king and the ministers are nothing but fools. They reverse the order of the day and night and punished anyone who violate the laws.

Concept note-5: -The old bricklayer was found not guilty because he claimed that he got distracted by the jingling anklets of the dancing girl who went up and down the street many times on the day the wall was built by him.
