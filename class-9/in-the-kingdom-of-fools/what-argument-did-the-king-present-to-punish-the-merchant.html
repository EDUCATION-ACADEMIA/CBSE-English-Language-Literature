What argument did the king present to punish the merchant?

(A) ** He inherited his father’s sin in the same way as he inherits his wealth

(B) He must sacrifice for his father’s sake

(C) He must accomplish his father’s half done works

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: The king wanted to punish the merchant because the wall of his house and it fell on the thief killing him. Since the merchant had inherited his father’s wealth he inherited his sins too therefore, he should be punished.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The merchant was finally found guilty of killing the thief. He was ordered to be executed. But he was too thin to fit the newly made stake.

Concept note-3: -Answer: A man who become fat because of eating.

Concept note-4: -IN the Kingdom of Fools, both the king and the minister were idiots. They didn’t want to run things like other kings, so they decided to change night into day and day into night.

Concept note-5: -The people did not go against the order of the king and the minister because of fear of death. They had been told that anyone who disobeyed would be punished with death.
