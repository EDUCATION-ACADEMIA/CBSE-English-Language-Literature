IN THE KINGDOM OF FOOLS

[SOURCES]
What did the guru and his disciple find at the grocery shop?

(A) many beautiful things

(B) ** same price for all the things

(C) a duddu

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To their astonishment, they found that everything cost the same, a single duddu-whether they bought a measure of rice or a bunch of bananas, it cost a duddu. The guru and his disciple were delighted. They had never heard of anything like this.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: The guru and his disciple felt very hungry because they roamed in the city from morning till evening. When they saw the shops were opened in the nighttime, the two of them went to buy some groceries. They were astonished when they heard the prices of the food item.

Concept note-3: -The two strange things that the guru and his disciple found in the kingdom of fools were that the whole kingdom was asleep during the day and carry out their work at night and whether it was a measure of rice or a bunch of banana everything cost the same in the kingdom one duddu one rupee.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The Guru told his disciple that it was dangerous to stay in a Kingdom of fools. He said that there was no justice only foolish, unpredictable behaviour.
