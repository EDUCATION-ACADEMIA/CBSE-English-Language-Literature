On which day did the fire accident take Place?

(A) Saturday

(B) ** Sunday

(C) Monday

(D) Tuesday

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On which day did the fire accident take Place? a)Saturdayb)Sundayc)Mondayd)TuesdayCorrect answer is option ‘B’.

Concept note-2: -To add to his miseries, one Sunday afternoon, his house caught fire. Other than a few papers and photos of his father that were retrieved by his mother, they lost everything. Zan did not even have a pair of shoes. His pet cat was also missing.

Concept note-3: -These accidents can occur from faulty wiring, defective products, discarded cigarettes left on flammable materials, and smoke detectors that fail to activate. Car fires can trap victims inside a vehicle. A major concern for anyone involved in a fire accident is the damage smoke inhalation can cause to the lungs.

Concept note-4: -On Sunday afternoon, the author noticed a weird smell. He saw fumes of smoke pouring in through the ceiling and filling up the room quickly. They ran out of the house in haste and saw the whole house ceiling was covered with flames.
