CLASS 9

A HOUSE IS NOT A HOME

[SOURCES]
Where did the author run when the fire broke out?

(A) ** to his neighbour’s house

(B) to his grandparents

(C) on the roof

(D) all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: (b) in the house of neighbours.

Concept note-2: -Ans. It was a Sunday afternoon of a cold and windy winter when the fire broke out. The author was at the dining-room table doing homework with the fire burning in the fireplace. His red tabby cat was lying over his papers, purring loudly and sometimes swatting at his pen.

Concept note-3: -The author also shared his memory of how his house caught fire once when he and his mother ran into the neighbour’s house to inform the fire department. Then his mother rushed to the home again to save the metal box, pictures, documents, and letters by his father. However, the firemen were able to save his mother.

Concept note-4: -In what condition does the author go to school after the fire incident? Ans: After the fire incident, the author goes to school wearing the dress that he had worn to church on Sunday morning and the tennis shoes that he had borrowed from his aunt.

Concept note-5: -Solution: In order to rescue the photographs and other mementos of the author’s late husband, his mother ran back inside the blazing house. She treasured these memories more than her own life. She raced back inside the burning house because she wanted to save all of his memories.
