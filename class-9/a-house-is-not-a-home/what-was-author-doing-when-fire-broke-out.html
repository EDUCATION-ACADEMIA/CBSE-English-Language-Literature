What was author doing when fire broke out?

(A) playing

(B) ** doing homework

(C) sleeping

(D) eating

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans: It was a Sunday afternoon of a cold and windy winter when the fire broke out. The author was at the dining-room table doing homework with the fire burning in the fireplace. His red tabby cat was lying over his papers, purring loudly and sometimes swatting at his pen.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer Zan was doing homework at the dining-room table with the fire in the fireplace blazing. Zan lost his books, uniform and shoes as a consequence of the fire. It almost took five hours to extinguish the fire.

Concept note-3: -Where did the author and his mother spend their night OM-the fire accident? Answer: (b) in the house of neighbours.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The author was deeply embarrassed the next day in school because he was not wearing the proper dress. He had lost everything in fire. He was still wearing the dress he had worn to church that morning.

Concept note-5: -Ans: After the loss of all his belongings in the fire that ravaged his house, Zan, the narrator, started feeling depressed and embarrassed. At this time, the friendly and helpful actions of his schoolmates changed his understanding of life and people and comforted him emotionally.
