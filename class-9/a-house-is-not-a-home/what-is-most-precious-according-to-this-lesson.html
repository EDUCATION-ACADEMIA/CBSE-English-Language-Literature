A HOUSE IS NOT A HOME

[SOURCES]

(A) a well furnished house

(B) ** bond of love and care amongst the members of a family

(C) a house

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We thought that everything was over when suddenly a black sloth bear came out panting in the hot sun. Now I will not shoot a sloth-bear wantonly but, unfortunately for the poor beast, one of my companions did not feel that way about it, and promptly shot the bear on the spot.

Concept note-2: -01 A House Is Not a Home This story reflects the challenges of being a teenager, and the problems of growing up. The author drives home the message that life without affection is useless. Her feelings of depression and insecurity were natural when she thought that she had lost everything.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: The words which express the author’s embarrassment in the lesson “A House is not A Home” are embarassed, geek and strange, and weird and backpack.

Concept note-4: -The author also shared his memory of how his house caught fire once when he and his mother ran into the neighbour’s house to inform the fire department. Then his mother rushed to the home again to save the metal box, pictures, documents, and letters by his father. However, the firemen were able to save his mother.
