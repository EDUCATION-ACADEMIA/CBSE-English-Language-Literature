Why was the author feeling shy in the school?

(A) he was not wearing shoes

(B) because he did not have his school bag and books

(C) his dress was casual

(D) ** All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The author was feeling shy in the school because He did not have his school bag and books, he was not wearing shoes and his dress was casual.

Concept note-2: -He feels lonely and lost in the new school. After the fire tragedy, he feels isolated and depressed at the loss of his cat and house.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: He felt strange because he was a fresher, all of his friends were in different school and he was all alone.

Concept note-4: -The author also shared his memory of how his house caught fire once when he and his mother ran into the neighbour’s house to inform the fire department. Then his mother rushed to the home again to save the metal box, pictures, documents, and letters by his father. However, the firemen were able to save his mother.

Concept note-5: -Solution : Since it was a new school, much larger than her previous junior high school, he felt uncomfortable. It felt weird to be a freshman after getting the advantages of being in the senior class in junior high.
