An area where people live outside the centre of a city: ____

(A) lively

(B) ** suburb

(C) neighbourhood

(D) residential area

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -suburb | American Dictionary an area outside a city but near it and consisting mainly of homes, sometimes also having stores and small businesses: [ pl ] Most of the people who live in the suburbs work in the city.

Concept note-2: -A suburb is an area of a town or city, a little away from Downtown, where there are fewer big buildings and mainly houses, schools and shops. Such places are called suburban areas or suburban districts.

Concept note-3: -Suburban areas are lower density areas that separate residential and commercial areas from one another. They are either part of a city or urban area, or exist as a separate residential community within commuting distance of a city. As cars became the dominant way for people to get to work, suburbs grew.

Concept note-4: -Outskirts describes the outer edge of a city or town, farthest from the center but still technically part of that place.
