What was it that put the American Physicists in an uproar?

(A) The discovery of the atomic bomb in Japan.

(B) The discovery of the atomic power in America

(C) ** The discovery of nuclear fission in Berlin

(D) The discovery of atomic bomb by Einstein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When the Nazis came to power in Germany in 1933, Einstein emigrated to the United States. Five years later, the discovery of nuclear fission in Berlin had American physicists in an uproar.
