A TRULY BEAUTIFUL MIND

[SOURCES]
Happy Prince:How may things did GOD ask his angels to bring from the city?

(A) 1

(B) ** 2

(C) 3

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When God asked one of the angels to bring him two precious things in the garden of paradise the angel brought the leaden heart of the prince and the dead bird. God proclaimed that in his garden of Paradise the little bird shall sing for ever more and in the city of gold the Happy Prince shall praise God.

Concept note-2: -The story ends with God asking one of his angels to bring him back “the two most precious things in the city"; the angel returns with the Prince’s indestructible heart and the dead bird.

Concept note-3: -The swallow was so precious that when it died and fell down at the Prince’s feet, the Prince’s leaden heart broke into two. When God asked one of his angels to bring him the two most precious things in the city, the angel brought him the leaden heart and the dead bird.

Concept note-4: -[“Bring me the two most precious things in the city, ” said God to one of His Angels; and the Angel brought Him the leaden heart and the dead bird.]

Concept note-5: -The precious things mentioned in the story are the dead bird and the leaden heart of the Prince. These two are precious as they sacrificed their lives to help others in their desperate times.
