Concept note-2: -The atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki were the detonation of two atomic bombs over the Japanese cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki on 6 and 9 August 1945 by the United States.

Concept note-3: -The Americans dropped two atomic bombs on Japan on 6 and 9 August 1945.
