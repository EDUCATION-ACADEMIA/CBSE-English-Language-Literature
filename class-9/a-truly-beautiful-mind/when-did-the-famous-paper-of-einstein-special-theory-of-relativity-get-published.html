When did the famous paper of Einstein “ Special Theory of Relativity” get published?

(A) ** 1905

(B) 1910

(C) 1901

(D) 1907

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 30 June 1905 the German physics journal Annalen der Physik published a paper by a young patent clerk called Albert Einstein.

Concept note-2: -Einstein first published his Special Theory of Relativity-which describes his revolutionary ideas about light, time and energy-in 1905.

Concept note-3: -In 1905, Einstein published two articles on the Special Theory of Relativity. He completed his first paper in June, on the properties of light and time.

Concept note-4: -The annus mirabilis papers (from Latin annus mīrābilis, “miracle year") are the four papers that Albert Einstein published in Annalen der Physik (Annals of Physics), a scientific journal, in 1905. These four papers were major contributions to the foundation of modern physics.

Concept note-5: -Explanation: Einstein published one of his renowned papers called “Einstein’s Special Theory of Relativity” in 1905.
