Concept note-1: -An eclipse of the sun in 1919 brought proof that it was accurate. Concept note-2: -On November 7, 1919 The Times of London reported, “Revolution in Science, New Theory of the Universe, Newtonian Ideas Overthrown, ” sparking a media frenzy that launched Einstein into the limelight virtually overnight.

Concept note-3: -In 1916, Einstein turned that realization into his general theory of relativity, which revolutionized the concept of gravity.

Concept note-4: -No, Einstein lived long after the end of the Scientific Revolution. He was born in 1879 and died in 1955. This makes him part of the modern era. However, Einstein’s work was inspired by work that was conducted during the Scientific Revolution.
