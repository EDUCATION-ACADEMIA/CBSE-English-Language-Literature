Concept note-3: -Answers. After leaving the school at Munich, Albert wanted to go to Milan in Italy, where his parents lived and get admission in an Italian college or institute and pursue studies in higher grade mathematics.

Concept note-4: -Ans: Albert was forced to spend six months in Munich alone.

Concept note-5: -Around a year after Albert was born, his father’s featherbed business failed and the family moved to Munich, Germany where Hermann went to work for an electrical supply company. Einstein spent his childhood and his early education in the city of Munich.
