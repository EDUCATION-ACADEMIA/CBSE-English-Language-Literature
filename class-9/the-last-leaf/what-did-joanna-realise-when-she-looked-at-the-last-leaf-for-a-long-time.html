CLASS 9

THE LAST LEAF

[SOURCES]
What did Joanna realise when she looked at the last leaf for a long time?

(A) She was wrong.

(B) She was foolish.

(C) She wanted to live.

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What did Joanna realise when she looked at the last leaf for a long time? She was wrong.

Concept note-2: -Behrman paints a realistic-looking leaf onto the wall outside Johnsy’s window-tricking Johnsy into believing that one leaf has improbably survived a storm-Johnsy realizes that her attitude has been unacceptable, and she regains her health.

Concept note-3: -Johnsy fell very seriously ill in November. She had pneumonia. She would lie in her bed without moving, just gazing out of the window. Sue, her friend, became very worried.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Sue felt extremely worried that the lead would fall off by the next morning, and if Johnsy saw that, she would not survive.

Concept note-5: -This story teaches us that we should never lose hope in life quickly. It also teaches us the meaning of true friendship. Behrman sacrificed his own life so his friend wouldn’t lose hope and recover from her illness. He goes out of his way to keep Johnsy’s hope alive.
