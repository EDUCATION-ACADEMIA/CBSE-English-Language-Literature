CLASS 9

THE LAST LEAF

[SOURCES]
A hungry cook

(A) verbal irony

(B) dramatic irony

(C) ** situational irony

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When something happens that goes entirely against our expectations, usually in a funny or twisted manner. A fire station on fire is a perfect example of situational irony. We would expect a place dedicated to fighting fires to be the least likely to be on fire, but the fire itself subverts our expectations.

Concept note-2: -Situational irony is a common literary device that occurs when a writer crafts a plot point/event that is the opposite of what the reader expected. It creates some kind of contrast that’s interesting and engaging.

Concept note-3: -An example of Dramatic Irony in the Hunger Games is when the Gamemakers decide to push the tributes together by using a large fire. Because of this, Katniss catches on fire and actually becomes “the girl on fire".

Concept note-4: -Situational irony is when the outcome of a situation is totally different from what people expect. This type of irony is a literary technique that’s riddled with contradictions and contrasts.
