What did Johnsy say regarding the leaves?

(A) She will go away when the last leaf falls.

(B) She will be healthy again when the last leaf falls.

(C) ** She will die when the last leaf falls.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“When the last leaf falls, I will die, ” said Johnsy with finality. “I have known this for the last three days.” “Oh, that’s nonsense, ” replied Sue. “What have old ivy leaves to do with your getting well?

Concept note-2: -hundreds of leaves Johnsy tells Sue she is counting leaves. She tells Sue that she will die when the last leaf falls. five leaves Johnsy thinks the last leaf will fall overnight, and then she will die, too.

Concept note-3: -Young, struggling artist Johnsy is down with a severe attack of pneumonia and her best friend and roommate Sue nurses her with care. The doctor gives her the best that modern medicine offers, but Johnsy’s condition deteriorates. She is convinced that she is going to die soon and she knows when.

Concept note-4: -Answers. Johnsy linked her death with the last leaf because the leaves were falling fast and there was only one leaf left she thought when it will fall she will even die .

Concept note-5: -Ans: Johnsy had lost charm in life due to pneumonia. She thought that when the last leaf fell, she would die. Behrman finally painted the masterpiece. The leaf painted by him saved Johnsy’s life.
