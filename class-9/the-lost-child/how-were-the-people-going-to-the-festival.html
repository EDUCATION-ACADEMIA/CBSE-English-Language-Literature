How were the people going to the festival

(A) Going on foot

(B) Riding on horses

(C) Carried on Bamboos and Bullock carts

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -d)All the aboveCorrect answer is option ‘D’. Can you explain this answer? People are going to the festival on their foot because if they ride horses their will be traffic in the fair. The people were going to festival by own foot .

Concept note-2: -Answers. The boy was attracted to the lights and the things that he sees in the fair. It was the time of Spring-Festival, and all the people in the fair were dressed brightly. Their dresses were as they wear daily but they were bright in colors and they wore new clothes.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The fair was certainly very popular with people in general because they had left their daily routine work, dressed up in their best and had come to enjoy the spring festival. They were with their families and crowded the stalls and rides displayed at the fair.

Concept note-4: -The Author Described the people going to the fair as the people were coming out of small streets and some are coming walking through long distances, some rod on horses, some being carried in bamboo carats and some on bullock carts.
