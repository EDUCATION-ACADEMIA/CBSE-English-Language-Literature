The meaning of the word ‘pleaded’ is ____

(A) ** requested

(B) ordered

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -to make an urgent, emotional statement or request for something: He was on his knees, pleading for mercy/forgiveness. She appeared on television to plead with the kidnappers. [ + speech ] “Give us more time, ” they pleaded.

Concept note-2: -: to entreat or appeal earnestly. transitive verb. : to maintain (a case, a cause, etc.) in a court of law or other tribunal. : to allege in or by way of a legal plea.

Concept note-3: -OTHER WORDS FOR plead 1 beg, supplicate. 2 reason.

Concept note-4: -Explore ‘plead’ in the dictionary. (verb) in the sense of appeal. Synonyms. appeal. ask.
