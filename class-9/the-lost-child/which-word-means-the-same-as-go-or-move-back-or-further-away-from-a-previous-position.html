Which word means the same as ‘go or move back or further away from a previous position’?

(A) linger

(B) ** recede

(C) suppress

(D) plead

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some common synonyms of recede are back, retract, and retreat.

Concept note-2: -Synonyms. recede, retreat, retract, back mean to move backward. recede implies a gradual withdrawing from a forward or high fixed point in time or space. retreat implies withdrawal from a point or position reached.

Concept note-3: -Definitions of move back. verb. pull back or move away or backward. synonyms: draw back, pull away, pull back, recede, retire, retreat, withdraw back away, back out, crawfish, crawfish out, pull back, pull in one’s horns, retreat, withdraw. make a retreat from an earlier commitment or activity.

Concept note-4: -verb (used without object), re·ced·ed, re·ced·ing. to go or move away; retreat; go to or toward a more distant point; withdraw. to become more distant.

Concept note-5: -To retreat or move backward. move backward. retreat. recede. retrogress.
