(A) A Cruel Ruler

(B) Showy

(C) Side By Side

(D) ** Stopping somebody/something from reaching their destination

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -to stop or catch something or someone that is on the way from one place to another so that it does not reach the intended place: to intercept a letter. The ball was intercepted by Grady.

Concept note-2: -transitive verb. : to stop, seize, or interrupt in progress or course or before arrival. : to receive (a communication or signal directed elsewhere) usually secretly. : to gain possession of (an opponentâ€™s pass) : to intercept a pass thrown by (an opponent)

Concept note-3: -to stop, seize, or interrupt while in progress or on course the cat was about to hightail it out the front door when I intercepted her. Synonyms & Similar Words. Relevance. blocking. grabbing.

Concept note-4: -[+ object] 1. : to stop and take someone or something that is going from one place to another place before that person or thing gets there.

Concept note-5: -1. Prevent, hamper, hinder, impede refer to different degrees of stoppage of action or progress.
