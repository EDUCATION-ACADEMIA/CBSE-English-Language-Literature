THE LOST CHILD

[SOURCES]
What did the child realize?

(A) The fair was charming

(B) the toys were attractive

(C) ** that parents are the most valuable asset

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The child was crying because he wanted things from the fair. In his excitement, he lost his parents in the fair. Then, he did not want anything from the fair and missed his parents. It was at this moment that he realized that parents are the most valuable asset.

Concept note-2: -Answer: (c) bond of love between child and parents is above everything.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The child finally decided to make a bold request to his parents that he wanted to ride the roundabout but when he turned to look at his parents he realized that they were not with him.

Concept note-4: -Answer: When he wishes to get a ride on the roundabout and calls for his parents he gets no reply. Then the child realizes that he is lost and separated from his parents.

Concept note-5: -Towards the end, I think that the child reunites with his parents. The man who found the boy crying picked him up and showed him around the fair to cheer him up. As the child only wanted his parents, I feel that the man would try to search for his parents and try his best to unite them.
