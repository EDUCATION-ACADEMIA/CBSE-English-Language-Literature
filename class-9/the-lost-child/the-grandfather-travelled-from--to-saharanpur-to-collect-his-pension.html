The grandfather travelled from ____ to Saharanpur to collect his pension.

(A) Dibrugarh

(B) Nainital

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Dehra Dun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grandfather had to leave Dehra Dun the next day to collect his pension in Saharanpur.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Toto was transferred to a big cage in the servants’ quarters where a number of grandfather’s pets lived together. However, Toto would not allow other animals to sleep at night. Therefore, grandfather, who had to leave for Saharanpur to collect his pension, decided to take it along with him.

Concept note-3: -Toto would not allow any of his companions to sleep at night, therefore, as grandfather was leaving for Saharanpur for some work, he decided to take Toto along with him so that he could watch him. The ticket collector insisted on calling Toto a dog as he did not qualify for the other categories.

Concept note-4: -But the monkey wouldn’t allow any of his companions to sleep at night; so Grandfather, who had to leave Dehradun next day to collect his pension in Saharanpur, decided to take him along. Explanation for the Above Passage – Toto was shifted to the servant quarter. He was put in a cage.
