CLASS 9

THE LOST CHILD

[SOURCES]
List the things that the generous man did after picking up the child?

(A) asked his name

(B) consoled & offered many attractive things

(C) asked his parents’ names

(D) ** all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The man in the shrine rescued the lost child. He lifted him up in his arms and tried to soothe him.

Concept note-2: -What did he offer to buy? A kind man in the shrine heard the child’s cry and lifted him in his arms. To pacify the wailing child, he offered to buy all those things that he had asked for from his parents – horse ride, a multi-coloured balloon, garland, snake-charmers show, and a sweetmeat.

Concept note-3: -’ The man tried to console him and took him to the roundabout. He offered to have a ride on it, but the boy did not stop crying. We can understand that the man was very kind and loved children.

Concept note-4: -Answer: because he is not big enough.
