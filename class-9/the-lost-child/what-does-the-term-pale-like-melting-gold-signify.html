What does the term ‘pale like melting gold’ signify?

(A) ** The jewelry made of gold.

(B) The sunlight.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Here is your answer, The flowering mustard field looks like pale like melting gold because the flowers in mustard field were full of golden colour and when they are waving it seems like gold is melting.

Concept note-2: -Definitions of field mustard. weedy Eurasian plant often a pest in grain fields. synonyms: Brassica kaber, Sinapis arvensis, chadlock, charlock, wild mustard. type of: mustard. any of several cruciferous plants of the genus Brassica.

Concept note-3: -The little boy compared the mustard field to the gold, which looked dull after melting.
