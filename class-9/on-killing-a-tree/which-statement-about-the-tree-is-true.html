Which statement about the. Tree is true?

(A) ** The bleeding barks of the tree will heal the tree of the tree is getting any damage .

(B) The tree was there before a hundred years

(C) That tree was planted by a child

(D) The tree was laying towards a river which has a lot of crocodiles .

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The bleeding bark will heal as ‘curled green twigs’ and miniature boughs of the tree will grow from the bark. These branches and boughs will expand and the tree will regrow to its full size again.”

Concept note-2: -Explanation: When a tree is chopped, its leaves sprout from the wounded bark and curled green leaves rises from close of the ground. Ans: (a) The bleeding bark will heal.

Concept note-3: -It absorbs sunlight, air and water for years. So, it cannot be killed by hacking and chopping. It causes pain but the tree does not die. Its bleeding bark heals itself.

Concept note-4: -It has absorbed sunlight, air and water like a thief in this process, it has grown like a giant. 2. How does the tree heal itself? Ans-The tree is equipped with a power to heal itself.
