How does the poet want the wind to come?

(A) Fastly

(B) ** slowly

(C) Turbulenty

(D) Calmly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The poet asks the wind to come slowly and softly but not quickly in a powerful manner and he also asks it not to tear pages of books, break shutters of windows, and not to scatter the pages.

Concept note-2: -Explanation of the Poem: The poet is talking to the wind. He asks the wind to come softly. So, he is saying that the wind should not be very strong, loud.

Concept note-3: -Solution : The poet asks wind to blow softly because he knows that a strong wind will cause a lot of damage to structures that are not very strong. It will break the shutters of windows, throw the books from the shelves, and tear their pages and bring rain.

Concept note-4: -”What does the wind do when its comes softly ‘’ is an English Poem and describes the impact of a soft wind also known as a breeze. The poem describes the many advantages and blessing of a breeze including how it can blow out weak fires and does not damage homes.
