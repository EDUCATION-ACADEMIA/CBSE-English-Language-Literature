Why do people winnow the grain?

(A) to clean the dust

(B) to remove straws

(C) to take out chauff

(D) ** All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -People winnow grains to remove straws, chaff, and dust found in grains there.

Concept note-2: -Winnowing is the method in which heavier components of the mixture are separated from the lighter substances with the help of wind. This method is used for separating grains from husk after the process of threshing.

Concept note-3: -Winnowing is used to separate heavier and lighter components of a mixture by wind or by blowing air. This process is used by farmers to separate lighter husk particles from heavier seeds of grain.

Concept note-4: -Ans :The wind God winnows means that nature sifts the weak things from the strong ones. Everything that is weak is tossed by the powerful wind and gets destroyed. Just like the winnower separates the grains of wheat from the chaff, similarly, the wind god separates the weak from the strong. 3.

Concept note-5: -⏺ winnowing is a method of agriculture where grain is separated from the chaff . ⏺separating lighter Husk from heavier grains such as wheat. ⏺separating seeds from other dirt. ⏺ separating stones on rice or grain .
