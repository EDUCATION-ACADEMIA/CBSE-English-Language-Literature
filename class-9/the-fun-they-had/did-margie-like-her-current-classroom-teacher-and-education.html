Did Margie like her current classroom, teacher, and education?

(A) Yes

(B) ** No

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Margie hated her school because she had a mechanical teacher. It was in her house. She was supposed to sit in that room alone to complete her home task or assignments. The part Margie hated most was the slot where she had to put homework and test papers.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Margie hated school because it was not fun. She had a mechanical teacher who used to teach her every day at a fixed time in her house. She hated the part when she had to insert the homework and test papers in the slot on the mechanical teacher.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Margie and Tommy had mechanical teachers. They were large and black and ugly and had large black screens on which all the lessons were shown and questions were asked. These mechanical teachers had a slot in which the students had to put their homework and test papers.

Concept note-4: -Margie and Tommy had mechanical teachers, computers, and telebooks. Their school had no separate building. The robotic teachers have a big screen showing lessons and test papers.
