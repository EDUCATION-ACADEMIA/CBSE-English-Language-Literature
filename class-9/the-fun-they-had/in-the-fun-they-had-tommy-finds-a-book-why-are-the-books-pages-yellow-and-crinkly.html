In “The Fun They Had, “ Tommy finds a book. Why are the books pages yellow and crinkly?

(A) The book is very cheaply made.

(B) No one has ever read the book before.

(C) ** The book was printed a long time ago.

(D) Someone was trying to hide the book in the attic.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tommy found a real book in the attic of his house. The pages had turned yellow and crinkly because it was at least two hundred years old. Moreover, it was all covered with dust as it had been lying in the attic for ages.

Concept note-2: -The pages of the real book were yellow and crinkly because it was an old book which existed nearly a 100 years ago when the books were printed inspite of being saved and shown in the television screen that tommy and margie had.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: a) they are the students where tommy’s and margie’s grandfather studied. b) the pages were yellow and crinkly because they were too old and was kept in one place for a long time.

Concept note-4: -the pages of real book was yellow and crinkly because it a old book of old times, it was of about 100 of years ago.

Concept note-5: -(5) What was the colour of the pages of the book, found by Tommy? Ans: – The colour of the pages of the book was yellow.
