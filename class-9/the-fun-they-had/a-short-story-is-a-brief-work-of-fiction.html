A short story is a brief work of fiction.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -short story, brief fictional prose narrative that is shorter than a novel and that usually deals with only a few characters. The short story is usually concerned with a single effect conveyed in only one or a few significant episodes or scenes.

Concept note-2: -A short story is a work of prose fiction that can be read in one sitting-usually between 20 minutes to an hour. There is no maximum length, but the average short story is 1, 000 to 7, 500 words, with some outliers reaching 10, 000 or 15, 000 words.

Concept note-3: -Sukumar Azhikode defined a short story as “a brief prose narrative with an intense episodic or anecdotal effect".

Concept note-4: -Fiction refers to any type of literature that’s created from the imagination and typically has a narrative. It describes imaginary events and people. Usually, this comes in the form of books and stories.

Concept note-5: -A short, amusing true story is an anecdote. You might come back from a crazy spring break with a lot of anecdotes to tell.
