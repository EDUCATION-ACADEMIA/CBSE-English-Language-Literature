The word ‘loftily’ in the chapter means ____

(A) ** in a superior way

(B) in a boasting tone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meaning of loftily in English in a way that shows you think you are better than other people: “Your views are too simplistic for me, ” said the young politician loftily.

Concept note-2: -loftily: in a superior way. regular: here, normal; of the usual kind.

Concept note-3: -high in the air; in an imposing or towering manner:The speaker raised his fist loftily skywards as he spoke, drawing forth cheers from the crowd.
