CLASS 9

THE LAKE ISLE OF INNISFREE

[SOURCES]
What does the poet hope to get there?

(A) ** Peace

(B) Wealth

(C) Friends

(D) Name and fame

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The poet wants to go Innisfree in search of peace.

Concept note-2: -The poet wants to go to Innisfree to enjoy its natural beauty. Its morning, noon, and evening are different from the cities where high-rise buildings touch the sky. He hears the pleasing sound of birds and gets peace of mind. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -The poet will experience ‘peace’ in the lap of nature at the Lake Isle of Innisfree. He will observe the beautiful trees, insects, birds, and bushes. The calm and cool atmosphere of the forest will give happiness to him. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -Answer: The poet will get peace from snow that the morning will bring with it. There will be snow everywhere, till the cricket sings. The poet will get peace from living all alone near the lake of Innisfree in a small cabin made of clay and wattles.

Concept note-5: -Answer. Answer: in the given lines the author says that he will have some peace in the lake Isle of innisfree as he has spent his childhood there and knows that the place sooths his mind and his body and is closely connected to nature.
