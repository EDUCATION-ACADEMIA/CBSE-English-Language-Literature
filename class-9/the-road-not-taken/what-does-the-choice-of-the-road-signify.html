THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
What does the choice of the road signify?

(A) Too many things

(B) Two paths

(C) ** Toughness of a choice and decision in life

(D) Signifies your future

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Explanation: Q-16 The choice of road signify that all the people chose the easiest way and very common people chose the road which has difficulties and they know how to overcome on that.

Concept note-2: -The Road Not Taken’, the road symbolizes our life. The poet says that the path that we don’t choose in our life is ‘the road not taken’. He describes his feelings about that choice that he had left in the past. The path which we have chosen, decides our future, our destination.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The choice of road signifies the decision one has to make in difficult times while walking through the path of life.

Concept note-4: -It signifies his risk-taking habit. The term road less travelled or taken refers to an uncommon life choice, one that takes one in a different way than other people. The meaning is that it is a decision that demonstrates originality and leadership. The poet wished to be distinct from people and take a risk.

Concept note-5: -What impact did the choice of the ‘other road’ make on the poet’s life? Answer-While making a choice of the roads, the poet took time deciding which one to take. The poet’s choice shaped his life in a different manner. He had a shade of regret about his choke.
