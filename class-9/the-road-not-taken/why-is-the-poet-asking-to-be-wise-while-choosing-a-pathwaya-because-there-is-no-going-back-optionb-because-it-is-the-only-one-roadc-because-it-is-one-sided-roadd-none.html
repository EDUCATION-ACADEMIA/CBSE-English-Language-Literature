THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
Why is the poet asking to be wise while choosing a pathway?A) because there is no Going Back optionB) Because it is the only one roadC) because it is one sided roadD) None

(A) ** A

(B) B

(C) C

(D) D

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -because it is the only one road.

Concept note-2: -Answer. Answer: The poet is asked to be wise while choosing the path in the lesson “THE ROAD NOT TAKEN” by ROBERT FROST because one could not alter or change the path after choosing one.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: The poet looked down the road as far as he could see because the road he was walking on that morning got diverged into two; he was in doubt as to which road he should continue walking. One of them was quite worn out; the other was grassy. The poet was making up his mind.

Concept note-4: -(b) The poet is at a point where he cannot travel on both the roads and has to make a decision. He looks down one as far as he can to help him make the decision.

Concept note-5: -The poet decided to take the path that was less travelled by the others because he wanted to do something different in his life.
