CLASS 9

THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
Which thing decides a person’s future according to this poem?

(A) ** the path one chooses to walk

(B) the path one leaves behind

(C) the regrets

(D) the success

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: He decides to take a path which is less traveled by and that makes all the differences in life.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer In this poem, the poet mentions that he was travelling on a road which was divided into two paths, he had to choose one of them. He chose the path which was less travelled. Hence, the path which we choose define us.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The overall tone of the poem is one of regret. He believes that at some time far in the future, he will still be thinking of his two possible paths “with a sigh”. He does not anticipate being any less conflicted then or any more satisfied with his choice.

Concept note-4: -Answer: the poem speaks about the choices made by the people.
