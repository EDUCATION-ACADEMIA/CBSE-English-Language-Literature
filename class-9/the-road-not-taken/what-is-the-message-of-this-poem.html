THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
What is the message of this poem?

(A) ** be wise while choosing and taking decisions

(B) two roads are confusing

(C) road is nothing but a pathway

(D) all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We should be wise while making a decision as we cannot undo them and our life is determined by our choices. According to the poet, he was strolling down the road when he came to a junction. He could only walk on one of the pathways. After much deliberation, the poet chose to follow a different path.

Concept note-2: -Answer. In the poem Be the Best of Whatever You Are by Douglas Malloch, the poet continuosly tells us to be the best in whatever we can be. We must never be ashamed of what we are, as no job is less or small. In this world every job have equal importance for the people.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Yeah people should be aware of taking correct decisions because once we have taken a decision it will have a great impact on our lives. In the poem ROAD NOT TAKEN poet chose the less travelled path and it made all the difference.
