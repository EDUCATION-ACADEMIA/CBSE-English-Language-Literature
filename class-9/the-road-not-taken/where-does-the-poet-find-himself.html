THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
Where does the poet find himself?

(A) on the road

(B) on a bus

(C) on a muddy road

(D) ** on a fork

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution : The traveller finds himself in the yellow woods at a point where the road forks into two. The problem that he faces is that he cannot decide which road to take to continue his journey since it is not possible for him to travel both roads at the same time.

Concept note-2: -Answer. Answer: the poet find himself between the tow road where two roads are diverging. he faces problem that on which road he travel.

Concept note-3: -The traveller finds himself at a point where two roads diverge. His problem is to decide on which road he should walk.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The poet comes to a fork in the road and decides to walk on the path that looks less walked on.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Once, the poet came across a junction when his path diverged into two. One road was well trodden upon while the other road was grassy as less people had travelled on it.
