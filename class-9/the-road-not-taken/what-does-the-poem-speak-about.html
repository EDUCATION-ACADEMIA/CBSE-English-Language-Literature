CLASS 9

THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
What does the poem speak about?

(A) about the plight of roads

(B) about the people

(C) about two roads

(D) ** about the choices made by people

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: the poem speaks about the choices made by the people.

Concept note-2: -The choice has made all the difference in his life. (v) The poet says “I took the one less travelled by, And that has made all the difference”. The difference is that he did not choose that way of life which most people generally choose to get easy success, fame and money in life.

Concept note-3: -What does the choice made by the poet indicates about his personality in the poem ‘The Road Not Taken’? Answer: Robert Frost chose the road which others avoided or seldom took. This indicates that the poet is brave and adventurous.

Concept note-4: -The poet had to make a choice between the two roads that diverged in front of him. One of the two roads was grassy, which meant that it was less travelled by and had scope for some adventure. Whereas, the other road was much travelled by and thus, was less grassy.
