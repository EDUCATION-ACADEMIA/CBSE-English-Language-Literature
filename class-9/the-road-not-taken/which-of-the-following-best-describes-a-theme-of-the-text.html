THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

[SOURCES]
Which of the following best describes a theme of the text?

(A) ** Sometimes one must make a choice without knowing if it will be best and live with the consequences.

(B) There will always be decisions in life, but one can return and make the decision differently if one makes a mistake.

(C) Following one’s heart is easier than following one’s head, but since decisions don’t really matter it is best to follow one’s heart.

(D) Making the most common or popular decisions ensures that one will take the correct path through life.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following choices BEST describes what a theme is? A theme is a life lesson that the reader can take away from a story or poem.

Concept note-2: -"The Road Not Taken:” Which of the following best describes a theme of the text? Sometimes one must make a choice without knowing if it will be best and live with the consequences.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following describes the main theme of the text? It can be dangerous to blindly follow traditions that one doesn’t understand.

Concept note-4: -Which of the following statements best identifies a theme of the text? People should get to know each other first before resorting to judgment.
